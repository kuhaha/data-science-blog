SINGLE CITY TIMESERIES
The data is available in daily_min_temperature_data_melbourne.csv file.
The original data can be found at https://www.kaggle.com/paulbrabban/daily-minimum-temperatures-in-melbourne.
The original data goes from January 1, 1981 to December 30 1990. 
However, in this post we are only taking data from  January 1, 1981 to October 9 1982 (just an arbitrary date!!).
